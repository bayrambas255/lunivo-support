Lunivo Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-26 Last updated: 2026-06-26 Version: 1.0

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") explains how the Lunivo mobile application ("App" or "Service"), developed and provided by Bayram Baş (individual developer) ("we", "us", "data controller"), processes your data. Please read this Policy before using the App.

⚖️ Legal notice. This text was prepared with reference to Türkiye's Law No. 6698 on the Protection of Personal Data ("KVKK"), the EU GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and COPPA. We recommend review by qualified counsel before publishing.

1. In short: your data stays on your device

Lunivo is an offline, device-first app. What matters is not how much data we collect — it's that we send none of your personal data to our own servers, because we don't operate any. Everything you enter and every record you create is stored only on your phone.

  • ❌ No account, no sign-up, no login.
  • ❌ No analytics, no tracking, no IDFA, no ads.
  • ❌ No third-party SDKs (no analytics / crash reporting / ad networks / social login).
  • ❌ Your data is never sold, rented, or used for advertising.
  • ✅ Data stays on your device; you can delete it anytime, and removing the App erases it entirely.

3. Scope of This Policy

This Policy covers the Lunivo app distributed on Apple platforms, all in-app features, and our informational web page (https://bayrambas255.github.io/lunivo-support).

This Policy does not cover the privacy practices of third-party services you may reach from the App (Apple App Store, Apple Health, external links you open). Review their privacy policies on their own pages.

4. Data Processed and Where It Is Processed

All of the following is processed and stored only on your device. We have no access to it.

4.1 Profile information you enter

DataWhereRetention
Name / nickname (optional)On deviceUntil you delete it
Age, sex, height, weightOn deviceUntil you delete it
Goals and plan preferencesOn deviceUntil you delete it

4.2 Records you create

Meal and calorie/macro logs, water and weight entries, habits, workout and walk records, pain/wellness notes, and daily history — all stored on your device.

4.3 Apple Health (HealthKit) data

With your permission, Lunivo reads your steps, walking distance, active energy, exercise, and weight from Apple HealthKit, and writes the weight you log back to Apple Health.

Important: Apple HealthKit data is processed on your device, is not sent to any server, is not used for advertising, and is not shared with third parties. You can withdraw permission anytime via Apple Health or iOS Settings.

4.4 App settings and notifications

Your preferences are stored on the device (UserDefaults). Reminders you set are scheduled as local notifications by iOS — no remote push, no APNs device token, and no server are used.

4.5 Face ID

If you choose to lock the App with Face ID, authentication is handled entirely by iOS and the device's secure hardware (Secure Enclave). We never access your biometric data; we only receive a "success/failure" result from iOS.

5. What We Do Not Do

We expressly state that Lunivo does not:

  • Send personal data to our servers (we operate none for personal data).
  • Perform analytics, behavioral tracking, IDFA collection, or cross-app tracking; therefore it does not request Apple ATT permission.
  • Show ads or use ad networks or data brokers.
  • Embed third-party analytics / crash-reporting / social-login SDKs.
  • Sell, rent, or share your data for marketing.

6. The Only Exception: Apple Platform Services

Even though your data stays on your device, the following limited interactions with Apple's operating-system services occur:

  • Apple App Store / StoreKit (Lunivo Pro purchase): Payment is processed entirely by Apple. We do not access or store your card number, bank details, or CVV. Apple only tells us your subscription status (active or not) and a purchase confirmation, which we use to unlock Pro features. For Apple's privacy practices: apple.com/legal/privacy
  • Apple HealthKit: On device, as described in 4.3.
  • External links: When you tap a privacy, terms, or support link, iOS opens your browser or Mail app; those services follow their own policies.

Apple is not a party to this Policy and acts under its own privacy rules.

8. Retention and Deletion

  • All data stays on your device until you delete it.
  • You can delete individual records from within the App.
  • The in-app Delete Data flow removes all of your data at once.
  • Removing the App deletes all Lunivo data from your device.
  • Since we hold no copy of your personal data, you do not need to request deletion from us separately. Purchase records held by Apple are subject to Apple's retention policy.

9. Your Rights

Under KVKK Article 11 and the GDPR you have rights to access, rectify, erase, and restrict processing of your data. Because your data is on your device, you exercise most of these rights directly within the App:

  • Access / rectify: From the profile and record screens.
  • Erase: Individually or all at once via Delete Data, or by removing the App.
  • Withdraw consent: By turning off Apple Health and notification permissions in iOS Settings.

You may still contact us: bayrambas255@icloud.com. We respond within 30 days.

10. Security

  • Data is stored on your device within the iOS app sandbox.
  • An optional Face ID lock provides additional protection.
  • Because data never leaves the device, risks such as server breaches or transmission interception are eliminated.

No method is 100% secure, but a device-first architecture aims for the lowest possible exposure of your personal data.

11. Children

The App is not designed for individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (we collect no personal data at all). If you believe personal information was entered on a child's device, you can remove it via Delete Data.

12. Automated Processing (Adaptive Plan)

Lunivo recalculates your calorie range weekly based on your own progress data on your device. This personalization happens entirely on your device; there is no externally shared profiling or third-party decision-making, and it has no legal or similarly significant effect on you. You can change your targets manually at any time.

13. International Data Transfers

Because your personal data never leaves your device, no international data transfer takes place. Purchases made through Apple are subject to Apple's own infrastructure and policies.

14. Cookies, SDKs, and Trackers

The App uses no cookies, tracking SDKs, or analytics tools.
